# MC Dean — I Built 63 Design Skills for Claude (Free)

63 skills across 8 plugins. MIT licensed. Install: /plugin marketplace add Owl-Listener/designer-skills.

8 Plugins: Design Research (/discover — personas, empathy maps, journeys), Systems (token audit, naming, a11y, theming), Strategy (/strategize — UX strategy, problem statements, metrics), UI Design (accessible palettes, WCAG AA), Interaction Design (state management, micro-interactions, user flows), Prototyping & Testing (/prototyping-testing:evaluate — heuristics, usability), Design Operations (/design-ops:handoff — developer handoff, edge cases, QA), Designer Toolkit (/designer-toolkit:write-case-study — portfolios, rationale docs).

Key insight: Skills encode how experienced designers reason, not just what they produce. Conditional application logic over theoretical exposition. Junior designers get scaffolding; seniors free cognitive resources for taste and judgment.

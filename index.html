The Core Problem

Distributional Convergence

LLMs trained on web data sample from the high-probability center. The result: Inter fonts, purple gradients, white backgrounds. This wiki documents how to break out of the default.

Without intervention

font-family: 'Inter'
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea, #764ba2)
border-radius: 16px
// every AI design ever

With the right prompting

font-family: 'Editorial New', serif
background: #faf6f1
border-radius: 2px
// distinctive, intentional
